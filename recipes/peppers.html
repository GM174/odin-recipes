Reliable Roasted Peppers

This healthy and vibrant dish can be your next go-to for a party appetizer or dinner with the fam.

Did you know that bell peppers contain more Vitamin C than oranges? These humble and affordable veggies will do the job of providing color and nutriants to your table. Prep time is minimal and perfect for a tight scedule. Nature's done most of the work with this one!

The source for this recipe is Shuan Brown who featured it on her blog last month. Shaun reasures those spicey-lovers that they can definatly kick this one up a notch and make it there own.

Ingrediants

  • 8 Bell peppers
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Tbsp Crushed Black Pepper
  • 1/2 Tbsp Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the peppers into quarters.
  2. Lay on a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle olive oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Roast at 450F for 25 minutes.
